üéØ Key UX Decisions

Design Principles & Rationale:

  • Mobile-First: Global travelers primarily use mobile devices while on the move
  • Intuitive Navigation: Clear pathways to discover, connect, and share resources
  • Community-Focused: Emphasis on profiles, connections, and user-generated content
  • Location-Aware: Geographic features to find nearby users and local resources
  • Trust & Safety: Profile verification and community guidelines to build safe spaces
